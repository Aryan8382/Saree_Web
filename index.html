How to wear a saree?

Traditionally, the saree was worn by draping it lengthwise around the body, creating many different layers and pleats. It is wrapped around an undergarment known as the "petticoat" or "silhouette", which is fastened around the waist, and a blouse, which is trimmed above the waist, leaving the midriff visible.
